FAQ

Common Questions

What is the minimum order quantity for wholesale?

Our Café Tier starts at just 5 units (1.25 kg). Retailer and Distributor tiers have higher MOQs but come with significant per-unit savings. Contact us to discuss your needs.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes. We send a complimentary 4-origin sample pack (approx. 60g total) to qualified businesses. Fill in the contact form and select "Free Samples" as your enquiry type.

Where do you ship to?

We ship to 28 countries worldwide. Free shipping applies to UK orders over 5 kg and EU orders over 10 kg. International rates are calculated at checkout.

Can I create a custom blend?

Absolutely. Our tea sommelier works with you to develop unique flavour profiles. Custom blending is available from the Retailer tier (20+ unit orders). Book a consultation to get started.
