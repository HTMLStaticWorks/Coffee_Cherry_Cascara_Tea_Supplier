Brewing Guide

Brew the Perfect Cup

Cascara is forgiving and versatile — brew it hot, iced, or as a cold-brew concentrate. Follow our guide for the best results.

Hot Brew Method

01

Measure

Use 15–20g of dried cascara husks per 350ml of water. Adjust to taste — more husk means more body.

02

Steep

Pour water at 85–90°C (not boiling). Steep for 4–6 minutes. Boiling water can make cascara bitter.

03

Serve

Strain into a glass. Serve with honey and a cinnamon stick, or over ice for a refreshing cold drink.

Cold Brew Variation

12-Hour Cold Steep

Add 25g cascara per 500ml cold water. Refrigerate overnight. Strain and serve straight, sparkling, or mixed into a cocktail.
