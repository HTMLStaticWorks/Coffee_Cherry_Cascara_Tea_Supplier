Who We Are

Crafting a Better Coffee Story

Founded in 2014, Cascara Luxe is on a mission to transform how the world sees — and tastes — the coffee cherry.

Origin

Our Tea Origin.

The coffee cherry has always been more than just a vessel for the bean. We saw opportunity where others saw waste — and built an entire supply chain around it.

Today we supply specialty cafés, wellness brands, and food manufacturers across 28 countries with the finest dried cascara and coffee fruit extracts on the planet.
